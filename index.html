WELCOME TO THE ARCHIVE
Welcome to my website!

This website is about the tobacco sponsorship era of F1, specifically the era spanning the early 1980s to 2005 when tobacco advertising money underpinned team budgets, but was restricted in some countries on the calendar.

When grands prix moved to these countries — France, Germany, Canada, and others — the cars appeared transformed: logos blanked, colours shifted, identities obscured. We call these the Sans Tabac liveries.

These restrictions defined an era of F1 aesthetics, leading to creativity from marketers and building team brands, especially for smaller teams like Jordan. That aesthetic is documented here.
